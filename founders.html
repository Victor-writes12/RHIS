Our Founders

Prince Aderemi Adekile

Founder

Prince Aderemi Adekile LL.B (Hons.), B.L, LL.M, ACIT, FCIT is the Managing Partner of Royal Heritage. Admitted as a Notary Public of the Federal Republic of Nigeria in 2004 by the Chief Justice of Nigeria, he is a legal practitioner of diverse practice backgrounds holding an LL.M in International Economic Law with a distinction in Company Law. He has worked at Irving & Bonnar, Bentley Edu & Co., Olakunle Orojo & Co., Kola Awodein SAN & Co., and Freshfields (Solicitors), gaining deep expertise in corporate law, oil and gas, banking, intellectual property, taxation, real estate and foreign investment. He founded Royal Heritage (Solicitors, Barristers, Notaries Public & Trade Mark Agents), establishing it as a leading commercial law firm in Nigeria.

Oluwakemi Adekile

Co-Founder

Oluwakemi Adekile LL.B (Hons.), B.L, LL.M, Ph.D is a Professor of Law at the Faculty of Law, University of Lagos, Nigeria. She gained extensive experience in general court work as Assistant Registrar at the Court of Appeal and later at Olawoyin & Olawoyin & Co., where she advised commercial clients on transactions with public authorities. She has vast expertise in negotiating local and international commercial contracts, advising foreign companies investing in Nigeria, and specialises in environmental law which forms a major part of her research and teaching.

Board of Trustees

Guiding the Institution

Sanusi Lamido Sanusi

Sanusi Lamido Sanusi, also known as Muhammadu Sanusi II, is a prominent Nigerian economist, banker, and the 14th Emir of Kano. He served as Governor of the Central Bank of Nigeria from June 2009 to June 2014, steering the nation's monetary policy through a period of significant economic reform.

Ukpata Victor

Ukpata Victor is a dynamic technology entrepreneur and digital innovator with years of hands-on experience in the technology industry. He brings a forward-thinking perspective to the Board, championing the integration of modern technology into education and institutional management.

Ajigbo Samuel

Ajigbo Samuel is a seasoned administrator and community development advocate with a strong record of leadership across public and private sectors. He brings deep institutional knowledge and a commitment to educational excellence to the Board of Trustees.
