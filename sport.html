Sports

Royal Heritage believes a healthy mind thrives in a healthy body. Students are encouraged to take part — and excel — in a range of sporting activities including football, athletics, volleyball, table tennis and more.

We hold regular inter-house sports competitions that bring the whole school together, building teamwork, discipline and school pride both on and off the field.
